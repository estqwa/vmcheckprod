# Reviewer Notes (EN) — QazaQuiz

## App Store Connect / Google Play Console

QazaQuiz is a free-to-enter, skill-based live trivia app.

Important review notes:

- Users do not pay money to enter trivia games.
- The app does not accept deposits, wagers, or paid entries.
- The app does not sell a better chance of winning.
- Winners are determined by gameplay results recorded by the server.
- Rankings are based on score, correct answers, and other disclosed game criteria.
- Prize-funded quizzes are sponsored by QazaQuiz.
- Official Rules are available in-app on the `Official Rules` screen.
- Participation in prize-funded quizzes is limited to eligible users only.
- Age, residency, verification, anti-fraud, and other eligibility checks apply before prize payout.
- Apple, Google, and their affiliates are not sponsors of these contests and are not responsible for them.

## Suggested short note for submission form

This app provides free-to-enter, skill-based live trivia games. Users do not pay deposits, wagers, or paid entry fees, and purchases do not improve their chances of winning. Prize-funded quizzes are sponsored by QazaQuiz and are governed by in-app Official Rules. Prize payouts are available only to eligible users after verification and anti-fraud checks.
